Igcabugao Cave

LocationBarangay Igcabugao
Distance14 kilometers from Poblacion
GuideYes
Itinerary*Php 450.00

To reach the cave you will experience mountain climbing and rock hopping. Along your way you can have a little relaxation in the cool and clean water of Igbolo creek. The chamber inside is as big as the church with the formation of stalactites, stalagmites, rimstone and flowstones.

* Itinerary may vary, please coordinate with our Municipal Tourism Office for more information.

Mt. Napulak

LocationBarangay Tigbanaba
Distance60 kilometers from Iloilo City
GuideYes
Itinerary*Php 700.00

The only mountain in the country shaped like a woman's breast, believed to be the last sanctuary of Marikudo, the Ati chieftain and his wife Maniwangtiwang. Enjoy bouldering at the giant granite stone at the top and view the glittering Iloilo City and cool sunset of the Province of Antique.

* Itinerary may vary, please coordinate with our Municipal Tourism Office for more information.

Kulapnitan Cave

LocationBarangay Passi
Distance1 kilometers from barangay proper
GuideYes
Itinerary*Php 150.00

Otherwise known as the bat cave. Different species of bats can be found inside the cave. It has a large deposit of guano, a first class organic fertilizer. It is located more or less 1 kilometer from barangay proper trelling along Tangyan River.

* Itinerary may vary, please coordinate with our Municipal Tourism Office for more information.

Nadsadjan Falls

LocationBarangay Passi
Distance1.5 kilometers walk from barangay proper
GuideYes
Itinerary*Php 150.00

A 100-foot high falls dropping on a giant cauldron-like natural swimming pool with its cool and fresh Prussians blue water. From Brgy. Passi, visitors may experience adventures trekking along Igbolo Creek including rock hopping and climbing to reach the place.

* Itinerary may vary, please coordinate with our Municipal Tourism Office for more information.

Lapusan Cave

LocationBarangay Igcabugao
Distance14 kilometers from Poblacion
GuideYes
Itinerary*Php 300.00

A cave in Barangay Igcabugao with underground river joining the Igbolo Creek. A 20-minute walk from barngay proper, you will enjoy the refreshing coldness of water that flows inside the cave.

* Itinerary may vary, please coordinate with our Municipal Tourism Office for more information.
